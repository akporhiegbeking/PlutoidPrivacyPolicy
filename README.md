# Plutoid – Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** February 5, 2026  
**Contact:** plutoidapp@gmail.com

Plutoid (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a product of **Plutoid Technologies Limited**. This policy outlines our commitment to data protection under NDPR, GDPR, and CCPA standards.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Account Data:** Full name, email address (verified via OTP), username, and password.
* **Professional Data:** Category and business contact info (for professional accounts).
* **Payment Information:** When subscriptions are active, transactions are handled by **Paystack**. We comply with PCI-DSS standards and do not store raw card information.

## 2. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
To maintain a safe community, users are prohibited from:
* Coordinating illegal financial schemes or fraudulent transactions.
* Harassment, bullying, or sharing prohibited content.
* Exploiting software vulnerabilities to bypass payment gates.

## 3. Data Security & Rights
We implement encryption and restricted access to protect your data. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us at the email provided above.

## 4. Child Safety
Children under 13 are not permitted to use Plutoid without parental consent (COPPA-compliant). We use AI and human moderation to ensure community safety.
