## PART 1: PRIVACY POLICY

*The Plutoid App is operated by Plutoid Technologies Limited. Below is our plain-English breakdown of how we collect, store, and process your personal data in accordance with the NDPA 2023.*

### 1. Data We Collect & Lawful Bases of Processing
Under Section 24 of the NDPA 2023, data processing must stand on a legitimate legal foundation. Here is what we collect, and why:

*   **Account Basics:** Name, username, email, and password hashes (secured via Firebase Auth).  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Performance of a Contract* (NDPA Sec. 25(1)(b)) – necessary to set up and run your account.
*   **Categories & Interests:** Custom personal classifications (e.g., Art, Animals, Technology, Coding) that you choose to customize your content feed.  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Consent* (NDPA Sec. 25(1)(a)) – which you can change or withdraw at any time.
*   **Identity Avatars:** Profile pictures generated via the **DiceBear API** using a seed based on your username.  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Consent* (NDPA Sec. 25(1)(a)).
*   **Connection Metadata:** Device model name (via `expo-device`) and approximate country (resolved via IP address query through **ipinfo.io** at registration).  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Legitimate Interests* (NDPA Sec. 25(1)(f)) – to optimize regional app formats and ensure security.
*   **Your Content:** Posts, media uploads, comments, likes, saves, and sharing/reposts.  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Performance of a Contract* (NDPA Sec. 25(1)(b)) – to display your feed and interact on the platform.

---

### 2. Analytics Tracking & Retention
*   **Analytics Dashboard:** We collect view events (`view_analytics`), comment records (`comments`), and likes (`likes_collection`) to produce the engagement metrics visible to you on your personal Analytics Dashboard inside the Plutoid App.  
    * **Lawful Basis:** *Legitimate Interests* – displaying engagement data that helps you understand your content reach.
*   **Retention Period:** We keep your data only as long as your account is active or as required by law (e.g. metadata retention rules under the Nigerian Cybercrimes Act).

---

### 3. Cross-Border Data Transfers
The Plutoid App is hosted globally. Your profile information and posts are stored on secure Google Firebase Firestore and Cloud Storage server environments located outside of Nigeria (such as in the United States or Europe).  

> **TL;DR:** Though we operate from Nigeria as Plutoid Technologies Limited, our backend infrastructure is hosted abroad. We ensure that international processors maintain adequate security standards compliant with Section 41-43 of the NDPA 2023.

---

### 4. Children’s Privacy
In alignment with Section 31 of the NDPA 2023 and the Child’s Right Act of Nigeria:
*   Users under **18 years old** must use the Plutoid App with the guidance of a parent or legal guardian.
*   Children under **13 years old** are prohibited from creating accounts. We do not knowingly collect coordinates from children under 13. If we discover an account violates this age gate, we terminate it immediately.

---

### 5. Moderation settings & Safety Controls
*   **Filters:** You can toggle **Filter Explicit Content** and **Hide Sensitive Media** (which blurs images by default) from your Content Moderation settings inside the Plutoid App.
*   **Reports:** You can report any post for review (*Spam*, *Misleading*, *Unwanted Sexual Content*, *Anti-Social*, *Illegal*, or *Other*). Submitting a report alerts our human review team immediately.

---

### 6. Your Rights Under the NDPA 2023
Section 34 of the NDPA 2023 grants you detailed rights over your data. You have the right to:
1.  **Be Informed:** Request clear disclosure of how your data is processed.
2.  **Access (Download):** Request and download a complete archive of your stored data (`user_data.txt`) directly from the Account Settings menu.
3.  **Rectify:** Modify or correct your profile data at any time via the Edit screen.
4.  **Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** Deactivate your account in Settings (sets status to `"0"`, hiding your posts from others) or request a permanent wipe by emailing `plutoidapp@gmail.com`.
5.  **Restrict Processing:** Request that we limit how your details are used.
6.  **Data Portability:** Download your data in standard, readable layouts to transfer elsewhere.
7.  **Object:** Object to direct telemetry and automated processing.
8.  **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw your consent for categories, profile pic requests, or optional settings.
9.  **Lodge a Complaint:** If you feel we have breached your rights, you can file a complaint with the **Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)** (https://ndpc.gov.ng).
